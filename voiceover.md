# After-Hours Bot — Demo Voice-Over Script

## TIMING GUIDE
Total runtime target: ~90 seconds
Tone: Confident, conversational, not salesy. Like showing a friend something cool.

---

## [0:00 - 0:08] HOOK
"It's 9pm. A homeowner's kitchen drain is completely clogged.
They Google a plumber... and text the first one they find.
What happens next makes or breaks that business."

## [0:08 - 0:18] THE PROBLEM
"Most plumbers — most service businesses — are closed.
The text goes unanswered. The homeowner moves on to the next result.
That's a lost job. Probably $300 to $500. Gone."

## [0:18 - 0:30] THE SOLUTION
"This is what happens when that same business has an after-hours AI bot running.
Watch this — it's a real conversation, happening right now, with no human involved."

## [0:30 - 0:58] DEMO NARRATION (plays over terminal animation)
"The customer texts 'hi' — the bot greets them instantly.
They mention a clogged drain — the bot knows exactly what to ask next.
Name. Callback number. Best time.
All captured. All confirmed.

And here's the important part — the moment the lead is complete,
the owner gets a notification on their phone. Right now. At 9pm.
Name, service, phone number, preferred time. Done."

## [0:58 - 1:10] EMERGENCY HANDLING
"And if it's an emergency?
Watch what happens when someone texts about a burst pipe.
Immediate response. Emergency number. No hesitation.
That's the difference between a panicked customer going elsewhere
and one who feels taken care of."

## [1:10 - 1:25] THE PITCH
"This runs 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, for any service business.
Plumbers. HVAC. Dentists. Gyms. Contractors. Anyone who misses calls after 5pm.

Setup takes less than an hour. It costs them less per month
than losing a single job to a competitor who picked up."

## [1:25 - 1:30] CLOSE
"If you want this running for your business — or you know someone who does —
the link is below."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES
- Voice: warm, measured, slight pause after "Gone." and "Done."
- Terminal animation plays from [0:30] onward in sync with narration
- No background music needed — clean and professional reads better
- Captions recommended — most social video is watched muted
